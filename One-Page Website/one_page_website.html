Cat Fun Facts

  • There are over 500 million domestic cats.
  • Domestic cats usually weigh around 8 to 11 pounds.
  • Cats usually live around 12 to 15 years.
  • The longest living cat lived to be 38 years old.
  • Cats only meow at humans, not to other cats.
  • There are 44 breeds of cats.
  • Cats sleep for about 70% of their life.
